About PaperWala

I designed this product to help local newspaper vendors

My father like to read newspaper every morning. So he takes newspaper amd magazine from local newspaper person. He given his home address and the newspaper guy told him "every morning 9:00am you will get the newspaper". So given him advance money of full month to start the newspaper subscription. Two weeks was all good but from third week he getting absent. When my father call me then he told him "I am sick due weather change" then. after two days of absent he started agine. Then after some days later he was not picking up out phones. So then my father agian move to another newspaper person and he told him the same rule to take newspaper at home. Same thing happend with my friends father who lives in differnt Locations. This is my experience and got this idea to slove this problem.
